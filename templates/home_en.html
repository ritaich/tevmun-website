A little message from organisers

"Modern Leaders, Today's Decisions"

Dear prospective chairs, delegates, and guests,

Welcome to TEVMUN 2026 edition!

This year, we have centered our conference around the theme "Modern Leaders, Today's Decisions." We believe that the youth are not just leaders of tomorrow, but the decision-makers of today.

TEVMUN 2026 is designed to be a melting pot of ideas, bringing together passionate delegates from any point of the world to engage in high-stakes diplomacy, tackle our most pressing international challenges, and craft innovative solutions that resonate beyond the committee room.

Our goal is to help you sharpen the leadership skills, strategic thinking, and global perspective necessary to navigate in this complex world. We truly hope this conference helps you crush your future goals and gives you the tools to succeed. But honestly? We also want you to have absolute fun while doing it.

Don't miss out!

Conference Date: 4–6 September 2026

Payment Policy: To secure your spot, please ensure the Delegate Fee is paid within 48 hours of receiving your acceptance notification.

We are incredibly excited to see the impact you will make this September.

With love,
The TEVMUN26 organisers
