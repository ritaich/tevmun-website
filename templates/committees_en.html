Committees

Find the room where your voice belongs

Security Council

Under the UN Charter, this body holds the primary responsibility for the maintenance of international peace and security and is the only organ capable of issuing binding resolutions.

Economic and Financial Committee (ECOFIN)

Formally known as the General Assembly Second Committee, it deals with issues relating to international trade, global finance, and the realization of the Sustainable Development Goals.

Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

An inter-governmental body within the United Nations system responsible for strengthening the promotion and protection of human rights around the globe.

United Nations Commission on Science and Technology for Development (CSTD)

A subsidiary body of ECOSOC that provides the General Assembly with high-level advice on relevant science and technology issues to improve policy coordination.

United Nations Entity for Gender Equality and the Empowerment of Women (UN Women)

The UN organization dedicated to gender equality, established to accelerate progress on meeting the needs of women and girls worldwide.

World Health Organization (WHO)

The United Nations' specialized agency for health, acting as the directing and coordinating authority on international health work and public health emergencies.

United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP)

The leading global environmental authority that sets the global environmental agenda and promotes the coherent implementation of the environmental dimension of sustainable development.

INTERPOL

An intergovernmental organization that facilitates worldwide police cooperation and crime control to ensure a safer world through the exchange of data and technical support.

Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA)

A non-profit association that represents the interests of motoring organizations and motor racing globally, governing international motor sport and promoting safe, sustainable mobility.

Turkish War of Independence

A historical crisis committee focused on the diplomatic and military struggles between 1919 and 1923 that led to the establishment of the Republic of Türkiye.

Game of Thrones

A specialized fantasy simulation centered on the geopolitical administration, power dynamics, and conflict resolution within the fictional continent of Westeros.
